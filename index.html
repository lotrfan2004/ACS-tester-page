ACS (Audio Charting System)

The ACS project has now closed, but if you'd like to see what we made back in Q1 2024 you can check out our youtube channel for a demonstration of the app!

What is ACS?

The ACS is an audio charting system that allows nurses to chart using only their voice. The ACS takes in audio recordings which are meant to be recorded while a nurse provides patient care. No matter how disorganized, it organizes them into clear sections such as assessment data, patient care, intake and output, etc. This allows nurses to record EVERYTHING they do without ever logging into a computer, or hunting through flowsheets.

The nurse simply hits the record button and says "I am entering room XXX" and then simply speaks all the assessment findings out loud- and the AI will organize it intelligently.
